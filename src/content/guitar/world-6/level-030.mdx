# Level 30: Barre Chord Quiz

## Lesson

Congratulations on completing World 6! You've mastered the essential **barre chords**: F Major, B Minor, D Minor, and learned to move shapes up the neck to play G and A. Barre chords are the "intermediate wall" that separates beginners from intermediate players. Once you can play these chords smoothly, you have access to any chord anywhere on the fretboard. The key principles are: apply firm, even pressure with your index finger, keep your fingers curved, practice daily to build strength, and use the sliding technique to transpose shapes. You've broken through the barre chord barrier!
