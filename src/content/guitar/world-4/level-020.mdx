# Level 20: Strumming Quiz

## Lesson

Congratulations on completing World 4! You've learned essential **strumming patterns**: Down Strum (DDDD), Down-Up (D-U-D-U), Country/Folk (D-D-U-D-U-D), and Pop Pattern (D-U-D-U-D-U). These four patterns form the rhythmic foundation for most guitar music. The key principles are: keep your wrist relaxed, maintain consistent timing, never stop moving your hand (especially in the pop pattern), and lead with your wrist rather than your entire arm. These patterns unlock thousands of songs across every genre from folk to pop to rock. This quiz tests your understanding of strumming terminology and technique.

## Exercise

Complete the quiz to earn your Strumming badge:
1. Review each pattern from Levels 16-19
2. Practice each pattern at its tempo
3. Focus on smooth, continuous motion
