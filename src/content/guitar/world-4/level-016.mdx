# Level 16: Down Strum

## Lesson

The **down strum** is the foundation of all guitar rhythm. In 4/4 time, you play one down stroke on each beat, creating a steady pulse. The pattern is simply **four down strums** (D-D-D-D) at a consistent tempo. The key is keeping your wrist relaxed and making a smooth, downward motion across all strings. Your strumming hand is like a pendulum—let it swing naturally. Focus on consistency rather than power. Every strum should sound even and connected. This builds your internal clock and prepares you for more complex patterns.
