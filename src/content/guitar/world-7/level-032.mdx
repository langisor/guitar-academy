# Level 32: Minor Pentatonic Pattern 1

## Lesson

The **Minor Pentatonic scale** is the foundation of rock and blues soloing. Pattern 1 is the famous "box" shape that every guitarist must know. The A minor pentatonic scale contains five notes: **A, C, D, E, G** (the root, minor third, perfect fourth, perfect fifth, and minor seventh). In Pattern 1, you play: open low E (muted), open A string, 2nd fret A string, 2nd fret D string, 3rd fret D string, 2nd fret G string, open high e. This pattern fits perfectly under your fingers and creates that classic rock sound when you bend notes. Practice this pattern slowly until it becomes automatic.
