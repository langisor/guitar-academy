# Level 35: Fretboard Quiz

## Lesson

Congratulations on completing World 7! You've learned essential **fretboard knowledge**: the chromatic scale with its natural half-steps (B-C, E-F), the minor pentatonic box pattern for soloing, interval shapes including octaves and power chords, and triads in all four types (major, minor, diminished, augmented). This knowledge transforms you from a rhythm player into someone who can navigate the entire neck. The key principles are: memorize the 12 notes in order, practice the pentatonic box until it's automatic, use octaves and power chords for rock rhythm, and learn triads for compact chord voicings. You've built the foundation for lead guitar playing!
