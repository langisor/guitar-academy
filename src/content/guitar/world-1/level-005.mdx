# Level 5: Beginner Basics Boss

## Lesson

Congratulations on completing World 1! You've learned the **fundamentals** of guitar: understanding the parts of the guitar (headstock, tuning pegs, nut, frets, fretboard, soundhole, and bridge), proper sitting posture to prevent fatigue and enable easier playing, standard tuning (E-A-D-G-B-e), and string names from thickest to thinnest. These basics form the foundation for everything else you'll learn. Take a moment to review what you've mastered before moving to World 2, where you'll learn your first chords. The investment you make now in proper technique and understanding will pay dividends as you progress to more complex skills.
