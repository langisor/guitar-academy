# Level 1: Parts of the Guitar

## Lesson

Learn the essential parts of your guitar: the **headstock** holds the tuning pegs, which adjust string tension for tuning. The **nut** sits at the top of the fretboard and guides the strings. The **frets** are metal strips that divide the neck into semitone intervals—pressing a string against a fret changes its pitch. The **soundhole** (on acoustic guitars) projects sound outward, while the **bridge** anchors the strings at the body and affects intonation. The **fretboard** is where your fingers press to play notes and chords. Understanding these parts helps you communicate about your instrument and maintain it properly.
