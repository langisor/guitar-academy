# Level 2: Holding the Guitar

## Goal
Learn the correct posture and guitar position for comfortable playing.

## Lesson

### Sitting Position
1. Sit up straight on a chair
2. Rest the guitar on your right leg (or left if you're left-handed)
3. Keep the guitar close to your body
4. Your back should be straight, not hunched

### Left Hand Position
- Your thumb should rest on the back of the neck
- Fingers should be curved, ready to press strings
- Position your hand near the frets (not in the middle)

### Right Hand Position
- Rest your palm on the strings near the soundhole
- Or use a guitar pick held between thumb and index finger
- Keep your wrist relaxed

### The Classic Mistake
Don't grip the neck too tightly! Let your fingers do the work.

## Exercise
Practice sitting with proper posture for 5 minutes:
- Check your back is straight
- Guitar is close to your body
- Shoulders are relaxed

## Challenge
Can you maintain proper form while counting to 60?
