# Level 2: Proper Posture

## Lesson

Sit with good posture and hold the guitar close to your body. Rest it on your right leg (or left if you're left-handed). Keep your back straight and shoulders relaxed.

## Exercise

Practice sitting in the correct position for 2 minutes.

## Tips

A uncomfortable position will make playing difficult.
