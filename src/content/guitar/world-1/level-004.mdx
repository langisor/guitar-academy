# Level 4: String Names & Numbers

## Lesson

Strings are numbered 1 (high e) to 6 (low E). Remember: Eddie Ate Dynamite, Good Bye Eddie.

## Exercise

Say the string names in order while pointing.

## Tips

The acronym helps remember low to high.
