# Level 7: C Major

## Lesson

C is a 'stretchy' chord. Keep your thumb behind the neck for leverage. Avoid muting the open G string.

## Exercise

Practice the C chord for 1 minute.

## Tips

The stretch between index and middle finger is the challenge.
