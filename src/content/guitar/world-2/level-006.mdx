# Level 6: G Major

## Lesson

The G chord uses 3 fingers: index on low E (3rd fret), middle on A (2nd fret), ring on D (3rd fret). The two high strings are open.

## Exercise

Practice the G chord for 1 minute.

## Tips

Keep your fingers curved.
