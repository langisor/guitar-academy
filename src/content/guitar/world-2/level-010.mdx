# Level 10: Open Chords Quiz

## Lesson

Congratulations on completing World 2! You've learned the **"Big Four" open chords**: G Major, C Major, D Major, and E Minor. These four chords are used in an enormous percentage of popular songs. G Major uses three fingers in a specific pattern on the lower strings. C Major requires a stretch between your index and middle fingers. D Major forms a triangle shape where you only strum the top four strings. E Minor is the simplest—you only need two fingers. Together, these chords unlock your ability to play thousands of songs. This quiz will test your knowledge of chord shapes and finger placements to ensure you're ready for World 3.

## Exercise

Complete the quiz to earn your Open Chords badge:
1. Review each chord shape from Levels 6-9
2. Practice forming each chord from memory
3. Test your knowledge of finger placements
4. Ensure you can identify each chord by its shape

Answer all questions correctly to unlock the next world.
