# Level 44: Travis Picking Master

## Lesson

Master-level Travis picking involves playing three-note patterns where your thumb hits three different bass notes per measure. This creates more rhythmic complexity and allows for elaborate arrangements that fill the musical space. The three-note pattern typically includes root, fifth, and octave bass notes cycling through. This technique is signature to players like Chet Atkins and Tommy Emmanuel who have perfected this style over decades. Practice this level only after you have mastered the basic and advanced patterns with ease. Focus on musicality and dynamics rather than raw speed—true mastery shows in the sound, not just the speed.
