# Level 43: Travis Picking Advanced

## Lesson

Advanced Travis picking incorporates chord changes within the pattern, requiring your left hand to switch shapes while your right hand maintains the alternating thumb pattern. This technique allows you to create full arrangements that include harmonic movement while keeping the signature syncopated feel. The challenge lies in coordinating two independent motor tasks—fretting chords while picking patterns. Start with simple two-chord progressions before attempting complex changes. Listen to Merle Travis, Chet Atkins, and Tommy Emmanuel to understand the musical possibilities. The goal is to make each note ring clearly while maintaining the driving thumb pattern underneath.
