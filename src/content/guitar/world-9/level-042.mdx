# Level 42: Finger Independence

## Lesson

Finger independence is the ability to move each finger on your picking hand independently without affecting the others. This skill is essential for advanced fingerstyle playing where you might need to sustain a bass note while playing melody on higher strings. Many guitarists develop habits that couple their fingers together, which limits their playing potential. Training finger independence involves exercises that isolate each finger and practice moving them in unconventional patterns. Start with simple exercises and gradually increase complexity as your muscles learn new movements. Consistent daily practice of even five minutes will show significant improvement over time.
