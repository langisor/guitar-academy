# Level 15: Transition Quiz

## Lesson

Congratulations on completing World 3! You've mastered essential **chord transitions**: G to C (using the middle finger pivot), C to D (ring finger slides), D to Em (lift two fingers), and Em to Am (move shape down a string). Smooth chord transitions are what separate beginner strummers from actual musicians. The key principles are: minimize finger movement, keep fingers close to the fretboard, use pivot fingers as anchors, and practice slowly before speeding up. These four transitions unlock countless song progressions. This quiz tests your understanding of transition technique and finger movements.
