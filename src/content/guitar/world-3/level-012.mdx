# Level 12: C → D

## Lesson

The **C to D transition** is a common chord change in countless songs. Notice how similar the finger positions are—your **ring finger** does most of the work. In C Major, your ring finger is on the 3rd fret of the A string. For D Major, it moves to the 3rd fret of the B string—a simple lateral shift. The index and middle fingers move from the B and D strings to the G and B strings respectively. Focus on keeping your ring finger as your anchor point, sliding it cleanly from one string to the next. This transition teaches your fingers to work together as a team.
