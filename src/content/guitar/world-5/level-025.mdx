# Level 25: Song Challenge Boss

## Lesson

Congratulations on completing World 5! You've learned to play four classic songs: **"Let It Be"** (C-G-Am-Em), **"A Horse with No Name"** (Em-D), **"Wonderwall"** (Em-G-D-Am), and **"Knockin' on Heaven's Door"** (G-D-Am). These songs represent essential chord progressions used in thousands of popular songs. By mastering these progressions, you've unlocked the ability to play countless other songs that use similar patterns. The key to learning songs efficiently is recognizing chord patterns and progressions. Now it's time to test your knowledge and see if you can identify which progression belongs to which song.

## Exercise

Complete the quiz to earn your First Songs badge:
1. Review each song's chord progression
2. Practice each progression until it's automatic
3. Test your knowledge of song-progression matching
4. Challenge yourself to identify songs by their progressions

Answer all questions correctly to unlock World 6.
