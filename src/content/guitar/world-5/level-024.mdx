# Level 24: Knockin' on Heaven's Door

## Lesson

**"Knockin' on Heaven's Door"** by Bob Dylan is a timeless classic that appears in films, covered by countless artists, and remains a staple of acoustic guitar playing. The progression **G - D - Am** (with the variation G - D - C) is beautifully simple yet emotionally powerful. This song has been performed by Guns N' Roses, Bryan Adams, and many others, showing its versatility across genres. The key to playing this song well is capturing its laid-back, reflective mood. Use the country/folk strumming pattern and let each chord ring out. The simple progression creates an evocative, meditative feel perfect for learning to express emotion through music.
