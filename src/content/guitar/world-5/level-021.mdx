# Level 21: Let It Be

## Lesson

**"Let It Be"** by The Beatles is one of the most iconic beginner songs in guitar history. The chord progression uses four fundamental chords: **C - G - Am - Em** (the simplified version replaces F with Em for easier playing). This progression appears in countless songs, making it an essential pattern to master. The song's message and melody are instantly recognizable, making practice rewarding. The key is maintaining a steady rhythm while smoothly transitioning between chords. Once you can play this progression fluidly, you'll have learned one of the most useful chord sequences in popular music.
