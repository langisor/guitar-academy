# Level 22: A Horse with No Name

## Lesson

**"A Horse with No Name"** by America is the **ultimate beginner song**—it uses only two chords: **Em and D**. This makes it perfect for new guitarists who want to play a real song quickly. The song has a distinctive acoustic feel with its laid-back rhythm and evocative lyrics about a desert journey. The progression is simple but effective, looping between these two chords throughout the entire song. Once you master the Em to D transition and the steady rhythm, you can play along with the original recording. This proves that you don't need complex chords to make great music.

## Exercise

Play through the progression smoothly:
1. Start with the E Minor chord
2. Count 4 beats, then transition to D Major
3. Count 4 beats, then return to E Minor
4. Use the pop strumming pattern: D-U-D-U-D-U
5. Practice the back-and-forth motion 10 times
6. Focus on the Em to D transition (lift two fingers)
7. Keep a steady, relaxed rhythm
8. Listen to the song and play along

Simple but satisfying once you get the rhythm.
