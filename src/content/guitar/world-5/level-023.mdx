# Level 23: Wonderwall

## Lesson

**"Wonderwall"** by Oasis is one of the most played acoustic guitar songs ever. The chord progression **Em - G - D - Am** is instantly recognizable to millions of fans worldwide. This progression uses all open chords you've already learned, making it accessible while still sounding sophisticated. The song's driving rhythm and memorable melody have made it a singalong favorite at parties, campfires, and gatherings. Every strum counts in this song—maintaining a steady, consistent pattern is essential. Once you can play this progression smoothly, you'll have a go-to song for any occasion.

## Exercise

Play through the progression smoothly:
1. Start with E Minor (2nd fret, G and A strings)
2. Count 4 beats, transition to G Major
3. Count 4 beats, transition to D Major
4. Count 4 beats, transition to A Minor
5. Use the country/folk strumming: D-D-U-D-U-D
6. Practice the full sequence 10 times
7. Focus on the transition timing between each chord
8. Keep your strumming hand moving continuously

Practice until you can maintain the rhythm without hesitation.
